## 🏨 ROOMS & LODGING

### Common Room

- **Shared sleeping space (bed or cot)**: 1 sp per night
- Most economical option for adventurers on a budget
- Access to the warm, lively atmosphere of the inn
- Shared with other travelers (typically 4-8 per room)
- Access to common well for fresh water

### Standard Chambers

- **Single Room** (for one traveler): 2 sp per night
- **Double Room** (for two travelers): 3 sp per night
- **Family Room** (for three to four travelers): 4 sp per night

All standard rooms include:

- Comfortable sleeping quarters with linen bedding
- Water pitcher and basin for washing
- Clay oil lamp
- Shuttered windows to keep out heat during the day
- Access to the common well for fresh water

### The Merchant's Suite

- **Two adjoining rooms with shared common area**: 1 gp per night
- Ideal for larger adventuring parties
- Includes private access to a small terrace overlooking the street
- Additional room available for equipment storage at 1 cp per night

### The Traveler's Respite (Premium Suite)

- **Four rooms surrounding a central courtyard with fountain and gardens**: 1 gp 5 sp per night
- Includes comfortable seating area with cushions and low tables
- Private access to the courtyard with water feature
- Best for merchant caravans or well-funded adventurers
- Fresh flowers and incense provided

- **Local Yeasty Brown Ale**: sweet, locally brewed — _1 cp per mug_

- **Dark Stout** (rarely stocked): _3 cp per mug_

### Wines

- **Red Wine** (table wine, dry): from the riverlands — _2 cp per mug_
- **White Wine** (cooler than red, slightly sweet): _2 cp per mug_
- **Palm Wine** (golden, sweet, potent): _2 cp per mug_

## 🎭 ENTERTAINMENT & AMENITIES

The inn features live entertainment every evening with musicians, storytellers, and dancers. No cover charge, but travelers are encouraged to tip the performers (1 cp to 1 sp per patron).

The spacious tavern is warm in the evenings and cool during the heat of the day, with plenty of room for dancing and celebration. Water pipes (hookahs) are available upon request for enjoying tobacco and spiced smoke (1 cp rental fee, tobacco extra).

## 💰 NOTES ON PRICING

All prices listed in **copper pieces (cp)**, **silver pieces (sp)**, and **gold pieces (gp)** per Pathfinder 1E standards. Thabat accepts coin of most nations, though foreign currency may be subject to a slight exchange fee at her discretion.

**A single traveler's budget for a night:**

- Common room: 1 sp
- Two modest meals: 4 cp
- Drink: 2 cp
- **Total: 16 cp** (roughly 1.5 sp)

**A single traveler's budget with private room:**

- Single room: 2 sp
- Two meals: 4 cp
- Drink: 2 cp
- **Total: 26 cp** (roughly 2.5 sp)

**A full party's budget (4 travelers) for one night:**

- Four common rooms: 4 sp
- Evening meal for party (moderate): 20 cp
- Drinks: 8 cp
- **Total: 5 sp 8 cp** (roughly 6 sp)
