---

## Overview

The characters came from far and wide to the city of [[World/Wati|Wati]], known as the Half-Dead City, in the southern country of [[World/Osirion|Osirion]] in the world of Golarion. They entered the Necropolis lottery and were granted rights to explore the ancient Tomb of Akhentepi — where their adventures began.
