# March 22 — The House of Penthru

**Campaign:** [[../index|Mummy's Mask]]

---

*As told by a storyteller at the fireside of a midevel pub — one installment of a longer tale.*

---

Gather 'round, friends, and let me regale you with the latest tale of the brave adventurers known as the BSGamers. This chapter finds our heroes delving deeper into the haunted estate of Wati, where dark secrets and ancient horrors lie in wait.

As the party made their way through the crumbling manor, they encountered a most peculiar sight — the ghostly vision of a young woman and her lover, caught in a moment of tender farewell. The vision faded as quickly as it appeared, leaving the group to ponder the tragic history of this place.

Pressing onward, they soon found themselves beset by a swarm of horrifying, floating heads — twisted, demonic things that sought to ensnare them with foul magics. But the BSGamers are no strangers to such terrors, and they fought with skill and determination, felling the abominations one by one.

The battle was not without its costs, however. The party's own Xandar took a grievous wound from the creatures — a bite that refused to heal properly. Try as they might, the group's healers could not mend the strange affliction.

Undaunted, the adventurers pressed on, discovering a hidden chest that proved to be a deadly trap. But their quick reflexes and sharp senses allowed them to avoid the worst of the danger, and they were rewarded with a trove of gold ingots for their troubles.

As they prepared to depart the haunted manor, the BSGamers heard the unmistakable sound of more foul creatures stirring in the shadows. Weary but resolute, they readied themselves for the next challenge, knowing that the secrets of Wati had only begun to reveal themselves.

The tale of the BSGamers' exploits grows ever more perilous, my friends. But take heart, for these are hardy souls, bound by friendship and a steadfast determination to uncover the truth, no matter the cost. The next chapter in their saga is sure to be one for the ages!
